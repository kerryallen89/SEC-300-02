## Course Stuff

### Course Objectives:

This course will introduce students to different quantitative risk analysis methods and techniques and help the students to identify the risk analysis process, which will help them to make informed and balanced decisions from the perspective of a Cybersecurity manager, director, and other leadership position.

### Course Outcomes:

* Be able to measure risk assessment methods in order to determine which models best fit a given decision-making situation.
* Be able to measure reduction in risk from a given defense, control, mitigation, or strategy.
* Be able to continuously and measurably improve on implemented risk measurement methods.
* Begin to implement more advanced risk measurement methods as threat landscapes continue to shift and adapt to current models.
